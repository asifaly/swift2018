MT 700 Field Specifications

1. Field 27: Sequence of Total

FORMAT

1!n/1!n (Number)(Total)

PRESENCE

Mandatory

DEFINITION

This field specifies the number of this message in the series of messages sent for a documentary credit, and the total number of messages in the series.

NETWORK VALIDATED RULES

Number must have the fixed value of 1 and Total must have a value in the range 1 to 8 (Error code(s): T75).
