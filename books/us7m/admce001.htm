MT 759 Field Specifications

1. Field 27: Sequence of Total

FORMAT

1!n/1!n (Number)(Total)

PRESENCE

Mandatory

DEFINITION

This field specifies the number of this message in the series of messages sent, and the total number of messages in the series.

NETWORK VALIDATED RULES

Number and Total must have a value in the range 1 to 8 and Number must be less than or equal to Total (Error code(s): T75).
